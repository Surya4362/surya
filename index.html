About Me

Analytical mindset.
Hands-on execution.

I'm a digital marketer focused on paid media — primarily Meta Ads and Google Ads — with solid experience across SEO, email marketing, and automation.

My approach is always data first. I don't just launch campaigns and hope — I track every key metric, read what the data is telling me, and iterate quickly. Decisions are grounded in numbers, not guesswork.
